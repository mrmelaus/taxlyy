Frequently asked questions
Got questions?
We've got answers.

Everything you need to know about Taxlyy Individual.

Is Taxlyy a registered tax agent?

No. Taxlyy is not a registered tax agent under the Tax Agent Services Act 2009. We provide an estimate report and lodgement guide — you still need to enter the numbers into myGov yourself. Think of us as your calculation assistant, not your tax agent.

🇨🇳 可以获取中文报告吗？Can I get my report in Chinese?

是的！Taxlyy 提供免费的双语报告（英文 + 中文）。 您可以在结账时选择偏好语言。报告中的所有内容都将以英文和中文显示。专为普通话用户设计，让您完全理解您的税务估算。

Yes! Taxlyy provides a free bilingual report in English and Chinese (中文). Select your preferred language at checkout. Everything in your report will be shown in both languages. Perfect for Mandarin-speaking users.

Do I get a completed tax return?

No. You get a personalised PDF report with your refund estimate and a step-by-step ATO lodgement guide showing exactly what numbers to enter into myGov. You still need to lodge through myGov yourself — but with our guide, it takes less than 5 minutes.

How accurate is the estimate?

As accurate as the information you provide. Taxlyy applies the official ATO 2025–26 tax brackets, Medicare levy (2%), Medicare Levy Surcharge, Low Income Tax Offset (LITO), and CGT discount based on your answers. The final assessment is always made by the ATO.

How long does it take?

Under 10 minutes to answer all the questions. Your report is delivered instantly after payment — no waiting, no appointment needed.

Do I need an account?

No account required. Your session is temporary and anonymous. No sign-up, no password to remember, no email verification. Just answer, pay, and download.

Is my data safe?

No permanent storage. All data stays in your browser session and is cleared after your report is delivered. We do not retain your tax information. Your TFN is always masked — only the last 3 digits are shown. Payments are secured by Stripe.

What tax situations do you support?

Supported: PAYG income (single or multiple employers), interest income, dividends & franking credits, rental property income & expenses, capital gains (with 50% CGT discount), work-from-home deductions, Medicare Levy, Medicare Levy Surcharge (MLS), Low Income Tax Offset (LITO), working holiday makers, and simple ABN sole trader income (business expenses).

Not supported: Complex business structures (partnerships, trusts, companies), BAS/GST lodgement, or complex business income. For these, please use Taxlyy Business (coming soon).

What are your pricing tiers?

Three pricing tiers based on your situation:

  • $69.99 – Single employer (PAYG only, one job)
  • $79.99 – Multiple employers (PAYG only, two or more jobs)
  • $89.99 – ABN / Sole trader (includes ABN income & business expenses)

Fixed fee, no surprises, no subscription. Your price is determined by your answers during the session.

Do I pay before or after?

You answer all the questions first, then pay to unlock your report. Your report is delivered instantly after payment.

I paid but didn't receive my report. What do I do?

Don't worry! Visit our Missing Report page, enter the email you used at checkout, and we'll resend your download link immediately.
